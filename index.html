Kerala: God's Own Country

Kerala, located on the southwestern coast of India, is often referred to as "God's Own Country" for its breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage. The state boasts serene backwaters, lush tea gardens in Munnar, and pristine beaches that invite relaxation and exploration. Visitors can enjoy the tranquil waters of Alleppey on traditional houseboats, drifting through interconnected lakes and rivers while witnessing the peaceful rhythms of life along the banks.

In addition to its natural beauty, Kerala is a cultural treasure trove. The state is renowned for its classical dance forms, such as Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, which bring ancient stories to life through intricate movements and vibrant costumes. Kerala’s colorful festivals, like Onam and Vishu, showcase its rich traditions and customs. The local cuisine, featuring coconut, spices, and fresh seafood, tantalizes the taste buds with dishes like Malabar biryani and Payasam. With its unique blend of adventure and tranquility, Kerala promises an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

Where to Go

Explore the must-visit destinations in Kerala, each offering a unique experience.

Alleppey

Known for its serene backwaters and iconic houseboats, Alleppey offers a tranquil escape into nature.

Munnar

Famous for its lush tea plantations, Munnar offers breathtaking views and , ideal for a refreshing retreat.

Varkala

A serene beach destination with stunning cliffs and natural springs, Varkala is perfect for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Jadayu Center

A unique adventure park that celebrates the legend of Jatayu, offering thrilling experiences and stunning views.

Fort Kochi

Rich in history and culture, Fort Kochi is known for its colonial architecture, vibrant art scene, and diverse cuisine.

Thekkady

Home to the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, Thekkady offers wildlife spotting, nature walks, and spice tours.

Resorts

Kumarakom Backwater Resort

Experience the enchanting beauty of Kerala at Kumarakom Backwater Resort, nestled on the serene shores of Vembanad Lake. Our resort offers a perfect blend of traditional Kerala architecture and modern comforts, ensuring a memorable stay. Each room and cottage boasts private balconies with stunning views of the backwaters and lush landscapes.

Indulge in authentic Kerala cuisine at our in-house restaurant, where fresh seafood and locally sourced ingredients are prepared with a touch of tradition. The resort also features an infinity pool overlooking the lake, perfect for a refreshing dip or a relaxing day in the sun. For the adventure enthusiasts, we offer kayaking, canoeing, and fishing excursions.

During your stay, take advantage of our rejuvenating spa treatments inspired by ancient Ayurvedic practices, designed to relax and revitalize your body and mind. The resort’s proximity to the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary makes it an ideal destination for bird watchers, offering guided tours to observe rare and migratory species in their natural habitat.

Forest Resort Wayanad

Nestled in the lush greenery of the Western Ghats, Wayanad Forest Resort offers a tranquil escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Our well-appointed cottages and villas provide panoramic views of the verdant landscape, blending modern comfort with rustic charm.

Engage in a variety of outdoor activities such as trekking through dense forests, bird-watching in the early morning mist, or exploring nearby waterfalls hidden in the wilderness. The resort’s eco-friendly practices ensure that your stay contributes positively to the preservation of the surrounding environment.

After a day of adventure, unwind with a therapeutic massage at our forest-themed spa, which uses organic, locally sourced ingredients. Our on-site restaurant serves a range of traditional Kerala dishes, crafted with fresh produce from our own organic garden. Evening bonfires, cultural performances, and stargazing sessions add a magical touch to your stay at Forest Resort Wayanad.

Tea Valley Resort Munnar

Nestled amidst the sprawling tea gardens of Munnar, Tea Valley Resort offers a serene retreat where nature and luxury converge. Surrounded by 55 acres of lush tea plantations, our resort combines contemporary comforts with the charm of colonial-era architecture for an unforgettable stay in Kerala's tea country.

Explore the tea estates with guided tours that take you through the history and process of tea-making, or simply enjoy a peaceful stroll through the rolling hills covered in tea bushes. Our spacious rooms and cottages offer spectacular views of the plantations, with large windows that invite the beauty of the outdoors inside.

The resort’s amenities include a well-stocked library, perfect for enjoying a quiet afternoon with a book and a cup of freshly brewed tea. For those looking to rejuvenate, we offer wellness activities like yoga and meditation sessions, set against the backdrop of the tranquil tea gardens. At our restaurant, savor fresh tea and local delicacies made from organic ingredients, ensuring a holistic experience that nourishes both body and soul.
